Mission

At PQTR, we work on a broad spectrum of areas spanning photonics and quantum technologies. Our research projects consider various aspects of these fields, including theoretical foundations, experimental setups, and practical applications. We aim to push the boundaries of science and technology through innovative and interdisciplinary research.

Please look at our publications for a feel of our current research directions. All credit for the work goes to a hard-working and dedicated group of students (and of course, our alumni).

Our lab is generously supported by various research grants and collaborations with academic institutions and industry partners. We are committed to fostering a collaborative environment that promotes innovation and excellence in research.
